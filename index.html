About Me

I am a Senior DevOps and Cloud Engineer with 8 years of hands-on experience designing, automating, and optimizing cloud infrastructure across AWS and IBM Cloud environments. My core expertise lies in Kubernetes (EKS), Infrastructure as Code (Terraform), and CI/CD automation, where I focus on building scalable, reliable, and production-ready systems. I have worked extensively on enabling teams to deliver software faster by streamlining deployment pipelines, reducing manual effort, and improving system observability. Over the years, I have designed and managed microservices-based architectures, implemented zero-downtime deployment strategies, and built monitoring platforms using Prometheus, Grafana, and CloudWatch. I actively work on improving infrastructure performance, ensuring high availability, and maintaining secure cloud environments aligned with best practices. I enjoy solving complex infrastructure challenges, optimizing cloud environments, and building systems that scale efficiently. My approach focuses on automation, reliability, and continuous improvement to deliver high-impact engineering solutions.
